# User Journey Maps

## 1. Importer/Buyer Looking for Suppliers

### Stages

#### Awareness
- **Touchpoints:** Social media ads, Trade fairs, Referrals
- **Actions:** Research online, Attend trade shows
- **Pain Points:** Confusing information, Lack of trust
- **Emotions:** Curious, Overwhelmed
- **Opportunities:** Provide clear, trustworthy information; Use testimonials.

#### Consideration
- **Touchpoints:** Website, Informational webinars, Case studies
- **Actions:** Compare suppliers, Attend webinars
- **Pain Points:** Difficulty in comparing options, Unclear pricing models
- **Emotions:** Interested, Frustrated
- **Opportunities:** Offer an easy comparison tool; Transparent pricing.

#### Decision
- **Touchpoints:** Website, Direct consultations, Reviews
- **Actions:** Request quotes, Consult with peers
- **Pain Points:** Delays in responses, Uncertainty in decision
- **Emotions:** Anxious, Determined
- **Opportunities:** Quick response system; Supportive resources for decision-making.

#### Retention
- **Touchpoints:** Email newsletters, Follow-up surveys, Loyalty programs
- **Actions:** Engage in feedback, Repeat purchases
- **Pain Points:** Lack of follow-up, Missed opportunities for repeat business
- **Emotions:** Appreciated, Neglected
- **Opportunities:** Regular communication; Incentives for returning customers.

## 2. Exporter/Seller Finding Buyers

### Stages

#### Awareness
- **Touchpoints:** Industry publications, Social media, Networking events
- **Actions:** Attend industry events, Research online
- **Pain Points:** Limited visibility, Competitive market
- **Emotions:** Hopeful, Anxious
- **Opportunities:** Create awareness through success stories; Collaborations.

#### Consideration
- **Touchpoints:** Product catalogs, Comparison platforms, Reviews
- **Actions:** Collect information on potential buyers, Evaluate options
- **Pain Points:** Unclear buyer needs, Difficulty in market segmentation
- **Emotions:** Cautious, Clarifying
- **Opportunities:** Targeted marketing strategies; Clear buyer persona documentation.

#### Decision
- **Touchpoints:** Demos, Product samples, Direct outreach
- **Actions:** Finalize deals, Sign contracts
- **Pain Points:** Negotiation hurdles, Pricing disagreements
- **Emotions:** Confident, Pressure
- **Opportunities:** Streamlined negotiation processes; Flexible pricing models.

#### Retention
- **Touchpoints:** Follow-up emails, Customer support, Feedback requests
- **Actions:** Build relationships, Repeat transactions
- **Pain Points:** Inconsistent communication, Lack of relationship management
- **Emotions:** Valued, Disconnected
- **Opportunities:** Personalized communication; Relationship-building strategies.

## 3. Data Analyst/Researcher Analyzing Trade Trends

### Stages

#### Awareness
- **Touchpoints:** Research publications, Conferences, Webinars
- **Actions:** Stay updated on industry trends, Attend research presentations
- **Pain Points:** Information overload, Lack of timely data
- **Emotions:** Curious, Overwhelmed
- **Opportunities:** Curated content; Regular data updates.

#### Consideration
- **Touchpoints:** Data platforms, Analytical tools, Networking groups
- **Actions:** Evaluate data sources, Compare analytical tools
- **Pain Points:** Tool compatibility, Inconsistent data
- **Emotions:** Analytical, Frustrated
- **Opportunities:** Offer comprehensive compatibility; Accurate data sources.

#### Decision
- **Touchpoints:** Consultations, Product demos, User reviews
- **Actions:** Choose tools, Prepare presentations
- **Pain Points:** Tool complexity, Cost of tools
- **Emotions:** Confident, Hesitant
- **Opportunities:** User-friendly solutions; Cost-effective options.

#### Retention
- **Touchpoints:** Training sessions, Updates, Community forums
- **Actions:** Continuous learning, Share insights
- **Pain Points:** Lack of support and updates, Community disengagement
- **Emotions:** Supported, Isolated
- **Opportunities:** Continuous support; Engaged community platforms.
